Citi ThankYou Points: Transfers, Travel & Credit Cards

Citi ThankYou is a flexible bank points ecosystem where rewards can be used for travel, statement credits, shopping — or transferred to airline and hotel partners. This guide explains how ThankYou points work and how credit cards accelerate earnings.

Best Cards for Earning ThankYou Points

1. What the Citi ThankYou Program Is

Citi ThankYou is a flexible points currency issued through select Citi credit cards. Depending on the card you hold, you can:

  • Earn ThankYou Points on everyday purchases.
  • Redeem points for travel, gift cards, or statement credits.
  • Transfer points to airlines and selected hotel partners.
  • Combine points from multiple Citi cards for higher value.

The strength of the program comes from its transfer partners — often giving far better value than fixed travel portal redemptions.

2. How ThankYou Points Are Earned

You can earn ThankYou Points through:

  • Citi credit cards – with category bonuses depending on card type.
  • Welcome bonuses – often the fastest way to build a large balance.
  • Travel and dining depending on your chosen card.
  • Online shopping and select merchant categories on specific Citi products.
  • Promotional multipliers Citi periodically offers.

Power users often combine multiple Citi cards to stack earning rates across daily spending categories.

3. How Credit Cards Boost ThankYou Earnings

Citi cards are the backbone of the ThankYou ecosystem. Depending on the specific card:

  • Higher earning categories such as dining, travel, transit or streaming.
  • Large welcome bonuses to jumpstart your balance.
  • Transfer access to airline partners (only on select cards).
  • Travel protections and perks varying by card tier.

The key is pairing the right Citi card with your actual spending categories.

4. Redemption Options for ThankYou Points

Citi ThankYou points can be redeemed for:

  • Travel portal bookings (flights, hotels, cars).
  • Transfer partners – often the highest value redemptions.
  • Gift cards across many brands.
  • Statement credits or cash-like options on certain cards.
  • Shopping portals including select online partners.

The ability to transfer points to partners is what makes ThankYou competitive with Chase Ultimate Rewards and Amex Membership Rewards.

5. Card Tiers Within the ThankYou Ecosystem

ThankYou doesn't have travel “elite status” like hotels or airlines. Instead, value depends on card type:

  • Basic ThankYou cards – earn points but cannot transfer to premium airline partners.
  • Premium ThankYou cards – unlock transfer partners and higher redemption values.
  • Co-branded Citi cards – add specialized spending bonuses.

To unlock the true value of ThankYou, you typically need at least one premium-level ThankYou card.
